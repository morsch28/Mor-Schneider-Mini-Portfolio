Connect Four

Connect Four is a strategic board game for two players. The board consisys 6 rows and 7 columns, and players take turns dropping a colored disc into a columns. The disc fall to the lowest available space in the choosen column or rests on the top of another disc if the lower space are filled. The goals of the game is to form a line four discs of the same color,either horizontally,vertically,or diagnoally
